Journey Through Childhood

Discover our age-appropriate programs designed to nurture your child's development at every stage

Bright Star

1 - 2 Years

The Bright Star Class is designed to help toddlers to socialize with peers with the help of caregivers and family as well as to have the confidence to try new things and explore their environment.

Sensory Play Social Skills Motor Development

Morning Star

2 - 3 Years

The Morning Star class is designed to offer exploration opportunities and social play with classmates, which will lay the foundation for future academic success.

Exploration Cooperative Play Language Growth

Sunflower

3 - 4 Years

The Sunflower class promotes letter recognition and phonemic awareness through a thematic approach that introduces children to alphabet letters using appropriate multisensory activities.

Letter Recognition Number Sense Thematic Learning

Lighthouse

4 - 6 Years

The lighthouse class is designed to emphasize phonemic awareness and alphabet recognition to build reading readiness skills. Valuable life skills will also ensure students to have a successful and positive foundation for all future learning.

Reading Readiness Life Skills Academic Prep

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, safe, and stimulating environment where children can develop their full potential through play-based learning, creative expression, and meaningful relationships.

Our Facilities

Discover our state-of-the-art facilities designed to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for learning and growth

Dream Tree Campus

Our beautiful, purpose-built campus provides a safe and inspiring environment for young learners. Featuring classical architecture with white columns and traditional Myanmar design elements, our facility offers the perfect blend of elegance and functionality for early childhood education.

Security features include 24/7 CCTV surveillance, fire prevention systems, controlled access gates, and trained security personnel ensuring complete safety for all children and staff.

Beautiful Architecture 24/7 Security Controlled Access Fire Safety Safe Environment

Well-resourced Library

Our library features extensive collections of fiction, non-fiction, and picture story books. The "Dream Tree Theater" corner provides an engaging storytelling space where children develop a love for reading and literature.

Fiction & Non-Fiction Story Theater Reading Programs

Meditation & Mindfulness Space

A peaceful space designed for quiet reflection, mindfulness activities, and cultural learning. This serene environment helps children develop emotional awareness and cultural appreciation.

Mindfulness Cultural Learning Quiet Reflection

Interactive Play Center

A vibrant, well-organized play space featuring educational toys, building blocks, dramatic play areas, and interactive learning stations that stimulate creativity and cognitive development.

Educational Toys Building Blocks Dramatic Play Learning Stations

Modern Classrooms

Age-appropriate learning environments with proper ventilation, natural lighting, comfortable furniture, and educational technology to support effective teaching and learning.

Natural Lighting Educational Technology Comfortable Furniture

Adventure Playgrounds

Safe, challenging outdoor play equipment designed to develop gross motor skills, confidence, and social interaction among children of all ages and abilities.

Motor Skills Safe Design Social Play
